Case Note

Editorial pacing that turns brand information into a controlled presentation.

The challenge was pacing. When a brand story relies on images, copy and supporting information, the design has to guide the reader instead of simply placing content on pages.

My role was to build a calmer editorial rhythm: image scale, whitespace, sequencing and production consistency that make the final presentation feel refined and ready to hand off.
