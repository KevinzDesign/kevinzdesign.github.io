Case Note

Making non-alcoholic beverages feel adult, intentional and shelf-ready.

The challenge was positioning. Non-alcoholic beverages need the sophistication of cocktail culture, but they still have to communicate quickly in a retail cooler or on a shelf.

My focus was building a flexible label system: flavor color, bottle/can architecture and mature visual cues that let each SKU stand alone while still reading as one polished beverage family.
