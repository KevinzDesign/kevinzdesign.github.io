Case Note

Turning complex wellness science into a clear retail system.

The work needed to make a technical benefit feel approachable. I treated the packaging as a system first: color coding, benefit hierarchy, product naming and format consistency all had to help a customer understand the family quickly.

The visual direction keeps the product elevated while still practical for production. Labels, cartons, cans and secondary formats share enough structure to feel like one brand, but each SKU keeps its own signal on shelf.
