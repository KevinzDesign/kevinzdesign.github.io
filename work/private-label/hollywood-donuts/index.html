Hollywood Donuts

A compact food-service identity system for donuts and coffee, designed to move from logo to packaging to merchandise without losing its personality.

RoleBrand Extension Design
ScopeIdentity, Packaging, Merch Design
CategoryFood & Beverage

Case Note

A local food brand with a simple, repeatable visual language.

Hollywood Donuts needed a mark and packaging system that could work on cups, boxes, apparel and small retail moments. The design keeps the palette tight and lets the emblem carry the brand.

The result is a straightforward brand kit: recognizable, useful and easy to reproduce across vendor files and production formats.
