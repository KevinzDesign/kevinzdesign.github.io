A rugged beverage identity built around a clear shelf signal, Americana cues and a direct voice that makes the product easy to recognize.

CategoryBeer & Beverage

Case Note

A beer identity built for quick recognition and repeat use.

Rodeo Cold leans into a working-class visual language without turning into costume. The packaging uses bold type, classic illustration cues and a restrained palette to create immediate category recognition.

The system is designed to stretch from label to truck graphic to product lineup while keeping production simple.
