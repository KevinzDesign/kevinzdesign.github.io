Case Note

A beer identity built for fast recognition and repeatable production use.

The challenge was authenticity. A rugged beer identity can easily become costume, so the system needed to feel familiar and direct without becoming overdesigned.

My focus was repeatable recognition: bold type, classic illustration cues, restrained color and layouts that can move from bottle label to truck graphic, product lineup and promotional material while staying production-friendly.
