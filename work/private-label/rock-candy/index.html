Case Note

A sensitive category translated into a polished retail sales system.

The challenge was category control. Adult-oriented products can easily become noisy or cheap-looking, so the work needed to feel energetic while still staying organized, professional and retail-ready.

My role connected the full chain: packaging design, product rendering, photography direction and brochure presentation. Bright color creates the product energy, while the layout system keeps the sales story clear and usable for real buyer-facing material.
