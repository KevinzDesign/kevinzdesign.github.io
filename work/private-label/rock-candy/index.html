Rock Candy

A complete retail presentation for a playful intimate product line, from packaging direction and product visuals to brochure-style sales material.

RoleEnd-to-End Creative Production
ScopePackaging, Product Visuals, Brochure
CategoryIntimate Lifestyle Product

Case Note

A sensitive category handled with clarity, color and retail polish.

Rock Candy is one of the strongest examples of an end-to-end project: product packaging, product rendering, photography direction and brochure presentation all working as one sales system. The goal was not to make the category louder; it was to make it feel organized, polished and easy to understand.

Because the category is adult-oriented, the design needs restraint. The page presents the work as product and retail communication, not shock value. Bright color carries the energy while the layout keeps the system professional.
