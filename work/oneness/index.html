Oneness

The Project

Science, translated for the shelf.

Oneness is a hemp-infused wellness system built around proprietary Phase-Shift technology. The visual identity turns a complex product story into a premium, approachable family spanning drops, sparkling beverages and gummies.

Scope
Identity, Packaging, Marketing
Category

Focus

The Visual System

One identity.
Distinct daily rituals.

Geometric linework establishes credibility, while benefit-led naming and color coding make each formula easy to recognize. The system stays consistent across formats without making the product family feel repetitive.
